Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions that come in your mind related to IPO.

How to Subscribe to an IPO?+
  • Step 1: Login to your respective service provider.
  • Step 2: Click on the IPO button.
  • Step 3: Select the IPO you want to bid and enter the relevant details.
  • Step 4: Your subscription will be completed once you make the payment or give permission.
Should I buy an IPO first day?+
It depends on market conditions and stock performance.
How do you know if an IPO is good?+
Research financials, demand, and company fundamentals before investing.
How to check IPO start date?+
Check the stock exchange or company prospectus for IPO dates.
What is issue size?+
Issue size refers to the total value of shares offered in an IPO.
How many shares in a lot?+
It varies by IPO, typically mentioned in the IPO details.
How is the lot size calculated?+
Lot size is determined by the issuer and market regulator.
Who decides the IPO price band?+
The company and its underwriters decide the IPO price band.
What is IPO GMP?+
IPO GMP (Grey Market Premium) indicates the estimated premium over the issue price.
How many lots should I apply for IPO?+
It depends on your investment strategy and IPO demand.
